IPHONE 15 PRO MAX

The iPhone 15, announced in September 2023, features a 6.1-inch OLED display with ProMotion technology for a smooth viewing experience. It is powered by the A17 Bionic chip, offering exceptional performance and energy efficiency. The advanced camera system includes enhancements for night photography and video. The iPhone 15 has a sleek, durable design available in multiple colors and supports modern connectivity technologies like 5G and MagSafe charging.
